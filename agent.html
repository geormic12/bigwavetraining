A Day in the Life

Here's what your agent might do on a typical Tuesday — without you asking.

7:00 AM
Checks your email. Drafts replies to 3 routine messages. Flags 1 urgent item and sends you a Telegram summary.
9:15 AM
You message it: "Research competitor pricing for X." It searches, compiles a summary, and sends it back in 10 minutes.
11:00 AM
Reminds you about a follow-up email you asked it to track. Drafts the email and waits for your approval.
2:00 PM
You say: "Build me a simple landing page for the new product." It creates the page, deploys it, and sends you the link.
5:30 PM
Sends you an end-of-day recap: tasks completed, items still pending, what's on deck for tomorrow.
Overnight
Runs scheduled health checks, organizes its memory, monitors anything you asked it to watch.
